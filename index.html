❓ HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Survive as long as possible while avoiding House Heads! The longer you survive, the better your time.

🎮 CONTROLS

📱 Mobile: Touch and drag the blue dot to move around

🖱️ Desktop: Click and drag with your mouse

🔦 Flashlight: Double tap/click anywhere to turn on/off

🏠 ENEMY TYPES

🏠 Small Houses: Fast but deal less damage (15 HP)

🏘️ Big Houses: Slower but deal more damage (25 HP)

Houses spawn → stay dormant → grow legs → hunt you down!

⚡ POWER-UPS

💚 Health Pack: Restores 30 health points

🛡️ Shield: Temporary invincibility for 5 seconds

⚡ Speed Boost: Move 2x faster for 8 seconds

🔦 FLASHLIGHT STRATEGY

💡 Light ON: See all nearby enemies clearly

⚠️ BUT: Enemies will hunt you aggressively!

🌙 Light OFF: Enemies wander randomly

⚠️ BUT: You can only see enemies when very close!

💡 PRO TIPS

  • Use the flashlight strategically - not constantly!
  • Houses bounce off each other - use this to your advantage!
  • Stay away from areas where houses are spawning
  • Collect power-ups to survive longer, especially health packs
  • Higher levels mean smarter, faster enemies - be ready!
  • Practice your movement - smooth controls save lives!
